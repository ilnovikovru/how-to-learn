The main problems in learning

Neither at school nor at the institute do they teach us how to study the material correctly. We are preparing for exams and learning tickets. We train to solve monotonous tasks in order to pass the test better, but Often, in the end, this does not give us real knowledge. Cramming wears off quickly and is not useful.

Conclusion: you also need to be able to study, but for some reason, few places teach this. What should I do about it?

Specific techniques and exercises help to change the approach to learning, to make its effective and exciting. The same techniques are used in the example of training in a workshop.

Teaching techniques

Five Practices from Barbara Oakley

  • Two kinds of attention

    Deep knowledge arises if you alternate between focused and distracted thinking.

  • Recall

    Remember what you have learned — this will allow you to connect disparate portions of memory.

  • Interleaving

    Learn several skills at the same time, they enrich each other.

  • Questions

    Listening to the teacher, come up with a good question that you really care about.

  • The illusion of competence

    Repeat to yourself, write it down, tell a friend: it only seems to you that you own a new topic.

For those who like to procrastinate

The story of Barbara Oakley.

Since childhood, Barbara has not been able to do mathematics. She considered herself finished a humanitarian, and far from the smartest. She studied Russian in the army to get a raise, yes so successful that she was promoted to commander. But in order to be promoted, you had to take math. And then Barbara came up with her own approach to the exact sciences. It turned out that if something is "not given well" to you, your hard-earned knowledge is much deeper than that of those to whom everything is clear at first glance.
